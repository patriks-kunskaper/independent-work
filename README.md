Self-directed security research and writing outside formal coursework: field notes, deep dives, and independent explorations.

A collection of self-directed projects, research notes, and technical explorations 
in cybersecurity and IT security. Topics pursued out of curiosity or personal interest, 
outside of formal coursework.

hands-on experiments, and analytical writing. No fixed scope — whatever the subject 
demands.
